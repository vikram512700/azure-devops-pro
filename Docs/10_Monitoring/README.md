# Monitoring & Observability Guide

This folder is about knowing whether the platform is healthy, degraded, or heading toward an incident.
It is written for engineers who need to make decisions from metrics, logs, traces, and alerts.

## What This Folder Covers
- Prometheus and Grafana
- Dashboards and alerting
- Health signals and SLO thinking
- Incident awareness and response

## Real-World Lens
- Monitoring is only useful when it leads to action.
- A useful chart tells you what changed and why it matters.

## Why It Matters
- Good observability shortens incidents.
- It gives you the proof you need to say the platform is healthy, not just up.
